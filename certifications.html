How to Verify any Company Registration

We encourage clients, suppliers, and business partners to independently verify details through the official CAC Public Search portal.

  1. Visit the CAC Public Search portal.
  2. Enter the company name or CAC Registration Number.
  3. You may also search using approved name or AV Code if available.
  4. Review the official registration details displayed by CAC.

The search results help confirm the company’s registered name, registration number, and other official records maintained by CAC.

What CAC Verification Can Confirm

A CAC search can help confirm important information such as:

  • Registered company name
  • Registration number
  • Entity type
  • Business status
  • Official records linked to the company

Why Verification Matters

Verifying a business before making payments or entering into agreements is an important due diligence step.

A CAC search gives an extra layer of confidence by helping confirm that a company exists as a legally registered entity and that the details provided match official records.

While registration alone does not guarantee performance, it supports transparency and accountability.
