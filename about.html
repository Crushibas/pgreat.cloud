About Graphkin Limited

At Graphkin Limited, we drive innovation at the intersection of spatial design, sustainable mobility, and industrial engineering. We are a multi-disciplinary solutions hub dedicated to providing high-value technical and creative assets that power modern environments and businesses.

Our design consultancy specializes in high-fidelity 3D visualization and interior space design. Utilizing industry-leading rendering software like D5 Render, we transform architectural concepts into hyper-realistic, immersive visual experiences. We work closely with developers, designers, and private clients to map out and perfect interior spaces long before breaking ground.

Parallel to our design studio, Graphkin is deeply committed to the future of clean, efficient transit. We specialize in sourcing and deploying premium e-mobility solutions, including electric bicycles and e-bikes. Operating on a dedicated, built-to-order procurement framework, we manage every stage of production and shipping within an efficient 60-day delivery window, ensuring our clients receive cutting-edge, factory-fresh technology engineered for performance.

To further support development and local manufacturing infrastructure, we also supply heavy-duty, high-precision block-making machinery. Available on a structured pre-order basis to guarantee exact technical specifications, our machinery—from specialized interlocking molds to standard hollow block systems—is tailored to deliver absolute structural reliability and operational efficiency for the construction industry.

At Graphkin, our diverse capabilities are unified by a single standard: precision, reliability, and forward-thinking execution. Whether we are rendering a luxury interior, deploying green mobility solutions, or powering production lines, we deliver the technical framework for future growth.
